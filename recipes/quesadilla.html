Quesadilla

Flamin' hot quesadillas!

Easy quesadillas with a dash of tabasco sauce. Simple and minimal.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Tabasco sauce

Steps

  1. Heat one tortilla on a pan with low-medium heat for a minute
  2. Sprinkle as much cheese as desired onto the tortilla
  3. Add a few (or many) drops of tabasco sauce over the cheese
  4. Place another tortilla over the cheese
  5. Let cook until cheese begins to melt slightly
  6. Flip quesadilla so that the other tortilla is on the bottom
  7. Cook until both sides are slightly crisped and cheese is melted
