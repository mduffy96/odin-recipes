Easy and Delicious Ramen!

A quick meal for any time of the day. Simply add an egg to liven up the meal with a boost in flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 brick of dry ramen noodles
  • 1 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 500 mL of water
  • seasoning
  • 1 egg (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil 500 mL of water in a pot
  2. Add ramen to pot once water begins to boil
  3. Let ramen cook for 2 and a half minutes, then lower the flame slightly
  4. Crack egg and directly drop it into the pot, with the boiling ramen noodles
  5. Let contents of pot cook for 30 seconds and then stop the fire
